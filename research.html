Research

Current Project

Somulating temporal and causal correlations with spatial correlations.

  • (i) Developing a Vorob'ev type result for temporal correlations arising from black boxes. Esentially, this allows us to say, given a memory function, whether nonclassical correlations can arise.
  • (ii) Showing that by flattening causal scenrios we can obtain contextuality scenarios. We can therefore simulate nonclassical causal correlatins with contextual ones.

Past Projects

  • (i) In the past I worked on modelling causal correlations in quantum mechanics by framing them as Nature and Experimenter games. Then, nonclassicallity arises because Nature does not use convex combinations of deterministic strategies.
  • (ii) I also worked on random spin networks, and modelling these when the number of spins becomes extremely large.
